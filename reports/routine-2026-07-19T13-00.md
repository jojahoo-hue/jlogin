## What Remains / Next Ideas

### High-priority generators to implement
1. **`sacred metatron`** — Metatron's Cube: 13 circles with all internal line connections, sacred geometry classic used in stencil art.
2. **`sacred mandala --petals N --rings R`** — Parametric mandala with configurable petal count and rings; would accept `--petals` and `--rings` args in the CLI.
3. **`plot harmonograph`** — Damped Lissajous (two pendulum simulation), produces organic oval-spiral curves; very Cricut-friendly.
4. **`plot spirograph`** — Hypotrochoid/epitrochoid parametric curves; classic and controllable with `--R`, `--r`, `--d` params.
5. **`render forest`** — Procedural forest silhouette PNG (fractal trees via L-systems or recursive branching); used as trace input.

### CLI improvements
- Add `--petals` and `--rings` flags to the `sacred` subparser
- Add `--mode` flag to `sacred flower` (outline / silhouette)
- Add `--R`, `--r`, `--d` flags to a future `plot spirograph` command

### Quality
- Stroke-width scaling at 1400 px produces slightly thin lines on some designs; could tune per-generator
- The `trace` output at 80 harmonics on the horse is detailed but introduces artifacts at joints — consider a `--smooth` post-processing pass
