**Potential next steps:**
1. **More sacred designs** — Metatron's Cube, Vesica Piscis, Torus knot; low effort, high visual value.
2. **More plot scribes** — Lissajous curves, Spirograph/epitrochoid, Van der Pol attractor.
3. **Color/fill modes** — Current output is outline-only; adding `--fill` and gradient support would expand Cricut use cases.
4. **Batch trace** — Accept a directory of images and produce an SVG per image; useful for Njaho's Congo art digitisation workflow.
5. **PNG export** — Optional rasterisation of final SVGs (requires Cairo or Pillow-SVG); useful for social sharing without a browser.
6. **`--harmonics` sweep report** — Auto-generate a grid of horse traces at 10/20/40/80 harmonics to tune the best trade-off between fidelity and path complexity for Cricut cutting.
