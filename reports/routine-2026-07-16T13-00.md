## What Remains / Next Ideas

### Priority: implement missing designs (referenced in spec for 4+ weeks)

Four designs have been called out in the spec every run but remain unimplemented. Adding them
is the highest-leverage next step for the project:

1. **`metatron`** (Metatron's Cube) — 13 overlapping circles in the Fruit of Life arrangement.
   Natural complement to `flower`, high-value sacred geometry target.
2. **`mandala`** — parametric with `--petals` and `--rings` flags. Highest Cricut stencil value.
3. **`harmonograph`** — Lissajous-family pendulum curves with decay. Numpy only, no extra dep.
4. **`spirograph`** — Hypotrochoid/epitrochoid curves. Distinctive SVG output; shares the
   `PLOTS` registry pattern.

### Other enhancements

- **`sacred flower --mode silhouette`**: silhouette mode only wired for `sri-yantra`. Extending
  it to `flower` would enable filled cuts of that design.
- **`render` diversity**: only `horse` exists. A tree, bird, or human figure would diversify
  the trace pipeline and produce different Fourier contour shapes.
- **`--name` flag on sacred/plot**: generators hardcode filenames; a `--name` override would
  allow rendering the same design at multiple parameter values without overwriting.
- **PNG export**: a `--png` option (or `rasterize` subcommand) would produce preview-ready
  rasters at target DPI without external tooling.
- **Batch `--all` flag**: regenerate every design in one command, useful after parameter sweeps.
