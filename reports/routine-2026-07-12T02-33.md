## What Remains / Next Ideas

### Missing designs (referenced in task spec but not implemented)
- **`metatron`** (Metatron's Cube): a high-value sacred geometry target — 13 circles
  arranged in the Fruit of Life pattern. Would complement sri-yantra and flower.
- **`mandala`**: parametric mandala with `--petals` and `--rings` control. Natural fit
  for Cricut stencil use cases (layered cutting).
- **`harmonograph`**: Lissajous-family pendulum curves with decay. Elegant and well-suited
  to the project's mathematical aesthetic.
- **`spirograph`**: Hypotrochoid/epitrochoid curves (Roulette family). Simple to implement
  with numpy, visually distinctive.

### Enhancements
- **`render` targets**: only `horse` exists. Adding other silhouette subjects (bird, tree,
  human figure) would diversify the trace pipeline.
- **`sacred flower --mode silhouette`**: currently only `sri-yantra` is tested in silhouette
  mode. Adding silhouette support to `flower` would enable filled stencil cuts.
- **Colour/stroke parameters**: all SVGs use default stroke. A `--stroke-width` flag and
  `--color` option would enable production-ready Cricut layer files.
- **Batch export**: a `--all` flag to regenerate every design in one command.
- **PNG export**: the trace pipeline produces SVG from PNG but no path back (SVG → PNG
  raster at target DPI for preview). Adding `--png` output would close the loop.
